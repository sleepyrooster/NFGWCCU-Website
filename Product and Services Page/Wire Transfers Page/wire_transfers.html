Wire Transfers

Conducting business with foreign companies often involves sending or receiving payments to or from other countries.

The most common method is Wire Transfer and foreign draft. Funds can be transferred in almost any major currency either by wire transfer or foreign draft. Our experienced officers can assist you in determining which method best suits your needs.

A Wire Transfer is the fastest method of transferring funds. With the use of wire transfers, you can usually make or receive payments within 48 hours. Wire transfers are convenient and can be handled through Bank of Saint Lucia.
