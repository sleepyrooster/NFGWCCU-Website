Fixed deposit is an investment instruments offered by NFGWCCU ltd, where members can deposit money for a higher rate of interest than Christmas saver. Members can deposit a lump sum of money in fixed deposit for a one-year period or rolling for multiplied years.

Features of Fixed Deposit
  • Fixed depositing members enable investors to earn higher interest on their surplus funds
  • Members can deposit money in a fixed deposit account only once, but to deposit more money, you need to create another account or wait till maturity
